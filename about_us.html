HR Assistance

Meet the team, read our stories and see our vision

HR Assistance was established in 2018, set up by it's co-founders bringing together their differing areas of expertise, combining to provide a HR Consultancy Service with a tailored and personalised touch.
We set out to ensure our customer base would have access to all the key elements of HR at their finger tips. Our aim has always been to not only help employers but also employees who may need support during challenging times. We have now grown our client base considerably having worked with individuals as well as businesses of all sizes spanning across numerous business sectors.
Our vision is to have a team of remotely accessible HR professionals, delivering support services to wherever you are in the world.

Hello, my name is Bobby, co-founder of HRA.

I have over 10 years valuable experience primarily in the area of employee matters, ranging from heavy involvement in managing and supporting businesses with both long-term and short-term absence issues, through to assisting and facilitating performance improvement plans and capability cases.

In addition to this experience, I am proud to be a Mental Health First Aider, skills learnt from which that I use to help both employees and employers with complex mental health cases.  

I passionately believe there is nothing more important to businesses than the staff it employs. Simply put, if your business looks after its staff, then the staff will look after your customers. At HRA we understand this is truer nowadays than ever before. As a result, we aim to help businesses keep their staff motivated and inspired through positive management techniques, understanding the importance of reviews and wellbeing meetings. 

Bobby Lock

Co-Founder

Hi, I’m Callum co-founder of HR Assistance.

I have an extensive background in dealing with both cases of a sensitive nature and high profiles matters, experience of which allows me to deliver expert advice as well as proactive forward planning to help your business learn and grow.

My speciality is formal process, policies and procedures. I believe HR assistance is like no other consultancy service, as we don’t just follow a generic approach, but we thrive to tailor our stance to help suit you and your business.  

I set up HR Assistance as I feel the industry is missing the personal touch. We don’t just offer advice; we home in on your personal skills and work with you to make those skills a leading industry strength. Together we help you and your employees live to work, not work to live. 

Callum Gallop

Co-Founder
