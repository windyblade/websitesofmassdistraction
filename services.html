HR assistance takes pride in providing quality support in all aspects of disciplinary action from providing advice on ad-hoc cases to facilitating and mentoring managers to hold formal hearings, whilst ensuring your business comply with all employment law regulations. We also offer recommendations on when and how to take formal action against an individual/s

Employee Relations

Absence Management
  • Absence prevention – understanding your people and having a resilient support function in place i.e. return to work meetings and well-being check ins
  • Case review – recommendations and advice available regarding any new or existing absence concerns you may have
  • Disability Discrimination Act awareness – supporting you on gaining an understanding of the Disability Discrimination Act and any other important legislation relevant to your work place
  • Long term ill health awareness – helping you learn how to manage LTIH, what it is and how to support the employee in returning to the work place
  • Policy creation & implementation – creating policies tailored to your business that suit your needs, making sure you have the right structure in place to allow your business to reach its full potential
  • Policy review & amendments – full detailed review of any existing policy documentation, feedback with recommendations and/or policy amendments including advice on trigger methods
  • Strategic planning – helping you to become pro-active with absence matters rather than reactive
  • Tackling mental health – providing assistance on how to handle and effectively manage an employee with sickness related to their mental health – qualified MHFA England
Performance Management
  • Under performance – we provide effective and useful performance management tools and methods to help you get the best out of your people.
  • Performance plans – Effective and useful performance improvement plans in place
  • Development plans – creating useful and powerful development plans
  • Retaining staff attrition – we can review your business model to help you retain good staff and help make improvements to your work place
The right conversations
  • 121 conversations – providing the tools to have useful and value adding 121 conversations
  • Appraisals/Performance reviews – tailored performance reviews/appraisals are key for any team member, we can help you have these and even have these for you.
  • Formal conversations – needing to have a difficult conversation or deliver a bad message, then let us help you do this.
Documentation
  • All types of documentation can be tailored and provided to any business of any level. We are qualified in professional writing, we can provide prompt, accurate and formal written and electronic communication, for any situation.
Case reviews for absence, performance and conduct
  • We know you cannot always plan for when situations occur, and when you need help you don’t want to be waiting for an appointment in 2 months time. That’s why HR assistance offer advice on a consultation basis 24/7.

Formal Guidance & Support

  • Disciplinary action – whether you are looking for advice on taking someone to disciplinary or are looking for someone to take care of the disciplinary action for you. HRA offer both of these services.
  • Mediation - do you have conflict between members of staff? Has their been a situation arise that you have chosen not to take formal action on, but feel the employees could resolve this on an informal basis. We can facilitate this for you, to ensure the conflict is resolved and the conversation is conducted in a professional manner.
  • Employee disputes - we can provide assistance on handling and addressing employee conflicts and making recommendations with an impartial view. Working with you to resolve in a timely and professional manner whether that is with an informal approach or formally.
  • Difficult/sensitive matters - we offer a strictly confidential service, which can be incredibly essential with difficult or sensitive matters. Of which will be handled under strict guidelines and with the upmost of care.
  • Pre/post employment tribunal – we are able to offer services for support on cases pre ET as well as support on implementation of recommendations post ET too.
  • Colleagues complaints/grievances – we take the pain out of colleague conflicts, by handling this all for you. We are able to facilitate and address the issues for you by providing an impartial input often this resolve the issue for you on an informal basis.
 Support
  • Review of current processes/procedures – HR assistance will undertake a full review of your company policies and procedures to ensure you are 100% HR compliant. We aren’t just here to point out errors, we can help you with making the changes too.
  • Support for employees – HR assistance are unique in the way that we do business, we aren’t just here for employers, we are also here for employees who are going through a difficult time. We can support you on disciplinary action, whether that be accompanying you as a representative or just some general advice prior to any formal meetings you are due to attend. We also support individuals if you feel you have been unfairly dismissed or treated in an unfair manner. HR assistance are here for all.
