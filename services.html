Absence Management
  • Policy review and amendments – full detailed review of existing policy documentation, feedback with recommendations and/or policy amendments.
  • Policy creation and implementation – creating policies tailored to your business that suit your needs. Making sure you have the right structure in place to benefit and reach full potential
  • Strategic planning – helping you become pro-active rather than reactive
  • Absence prevention – understanding your people and having a resilient support function in place I.e return to work meetings and well-being check ins.
  • Training and competency for leaders – providing a competency framework for your management team to develop and support their skills
  • E-learning sessions – we can provide a range of online material which can be delivered and used in your own time, ranging from “How to manage my wellbeing” to “Equal opportunity for all”
  • LTIH AND DDA awareness – providing a detailed training program on how to manage long term ill health, what it is and how to support the employee in returning to the work place. We also are able to support you on gaining a full understating of the Disability Discrimination Act and other legislation in place.
  • Tackling mental health – providing assistance on how to handle and effectively manage an employee with sickness, due to mental health.
Training & Development
  • HR Assistance offer a unique service, to support you and your leaders by providing a competency framework on holding formal meetings i.e disciplinaries, mediation and grievances. We coach you through each step of the way and will provide useful feedback on how to develop and become competent
Employee Support
  • Understanding relationships and dynamics – full comprehensive review of your organizational structure to help understand your respective responsibilities
  • Supporting work ethics and values – providing coaching on how to get the best out of your staff in a way that meets your businesses needs
  • Building confidence to build your business – providing HR support in a manner that enables you the time and space to focus on expanding your business
  • Understanding your people’s needs – support on how best to articulate and action suggestions from staff to help facilitate an open and honest, yet positive, business
  • Management training – providing training sessions on effective management, enabling you the skills to manage both over-performers and under-achievers
  • Team building – providing team-based activities to help get the best out your people and build positive relationships
  • Get to know your people –support on how to have value-adding discussions with your staff
Formal Guidance
  • HR assistance take pride in providing *quality* support in all aspects of disciplinary action from providing advice on adhoc cases to facilitating and mentoring managers to hold formal hearings, whilst ensuring your business comply with all employment law regulations. We also offer recommendations on when and how to take formal action against an individual/s.
  • Disciplinary action – whether you are looking for advice on taking someone to disciplinary or are looking for someone to take care of the disciplinary action for you. HRA offer both of these services.
  • Mediation - do you have conflict between members of staff? Has their been a situation arise that you have chosen not to take formal action on, but feel the employees could resolve this on an informal basis. We can facilitate this for you, to ensure the conflict is resolved and the conversation is conducted in a professional manner.
  • Employee disputes - we can provide assistance on handling and addressing employee conflicts and making recommendations with an impartial view. Working with you to resolve in a timely and professional manner whether that is with an informal approach or formally.
  • Difficult/sensitive matters - we offer a strictly confidential service, which can be incredibly essential with difficult or sensitive matters. Of which will be handled under strict guidelines and with the upmost of care.
  • Pre/post employment tribunal – we are able to offer services for support on cases pre ET as well as support on implementation of recommendations post ET too. Colleagues complaints/grievances
