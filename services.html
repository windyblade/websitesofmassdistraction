Onward Support Packages

Offering a variety of intuitive HR solutions as part of a support package aimed at providing you with the highest quality of onward support

  • Case Reviews – Recommendations and advice available regarding any new or existing concerns you may have
  • Phone Support – Access to telephone support with one of our HR Consultants, available 24/7
  • Email support – Unlimited email support available to help tackle and support with any concerns or queries raised
  • Consultant Visits – Consultant visit available to you and your business
  • Monthly Reporting – Root-cause analysis provided monthly, feeding back on any areas of development needed
  • Policy Review & Amendments – Full detailed review of any existing policy documentation, feedback with recommendations and/or policy amendments
  • Formal Documentation – Providing an array of documentation suitable for any formal situation such as invitation to disciplinary or outcome letters
  • HR Health Check – A 360° review provided of any of your current HR functions, such as recruitment, discipline and engagement
  • Formal Meeting Assistance – HR consultant available to assist in person on the day of any formal meetings carried out such as disciplinaries and grievances
  • Supporting Documentation – All management documentation such as return to work interview and 1-2-1 review templates made available upon request

Training Packages

A range of informative training packages all available on either a group or 121 basis

  • A Guide to Formal Procedures – A comprehensive training session providing a guide on how to undertake any aspect of all formal company procedures
  • Effective Performance Management – A training session delivered to help effectively manage the performance of both under-performers and over-performers
  • Effective Absence Management – A detailed training session supplied to help the management of absence, with both short-term and long-term absence covered
  • Mental Health Awareness – A training session delivered for all staff members designed to help raise awareness of poor mental health and the steps to undertake in the event of an incident

One-off services

Providing an array of professional one-off services suited to your business at your request

  • Disciplinary Support – Whether you are looking for advice on taking someone to disciplinary or are looking for someone to take care of the disciplinary action for you. HRA offer both these services
  • Formal Conversations – If you are needing to have a difficult conversation or are needing to deliver a bad message, then let us help you do this regardless of the circumstance
  • Minute-taking Services – Offering formal meeting minute-taking services if ever required
  • Colleague Complaints & Grievances – We take the pain out of colleague conflicts by handling this all for you. We can facilitate and address the issues for you by providing an impartial input
  • Difficult/Sensitive matters - We offer a strictly confidential service which can be incredibly essential with difficult or sensitive matters. Each situation is handled under strict guidelines and with the upmost of care
  • Tackling Poor Mental Health – Assistance provided by consultants qualified as Mental Health First Aiders (accredited by MHFA England) on how to handle and effectively manage an employee with sickness related to poor mental health
  • Mediation – Our mediation services aim to help resolve any employee conflicts as smoothly and effectively as possible ensuring each matter is dealt with professionally
  • Support for Employees – We are unique in the way that we do business in that we aren’t just here for employers; we are also here for employees too who may be going through a difficult time. Whether you require us to accompany you as a representative for a formal meeting or to provide just some general advice prior to any formal meetings you are due to attend. We also support individuals if you feel you have been unfairly dismissed or treated in an unfair manner. HR Assistance are here for all
  • Recruitment Solutions – We offer a full comprehensive range of recruitment solutions, ranging from sourcing new employees to assistance with the interview process
  • Performance Plan Support - We provide effective and useful performance management tools and methods to help you get the best out of your people

Management Documents

Offering a selection of effective and easy-to-use documents to help your business

  • Employee Contracts – Providing contracts of employment is essential to help provide a clear definition of rights and responsibilities between employee and employer. We provide employee contract templates and offer letters that can be tailored to your requirements
  • 121 Performance Reviews – Providing the tools and guidance to have useful and value adding 121 performance reviews
  • Appraisal Reviews – Appraisals are essential to help provide the opportunity to recognise and reward employees to ensure they feel valued by your business. We provide the templates needed to carry out these important value-adding reviews
  • Policy Creation & Implementation – Creating policies tailored to your business that suit your needs, making sure you have the right structure in place to allow your business to reach its full potential
  • Return to Work Interview – Supporting you with providing the tools and guidance needed to help keep your employees back at work following absence
  • Employee Handbook – It is crucial for a company to have an Employee Handbook as it helps to outline the expectations and values for employees. We can provide personalised Employee Handbooks of the highest quality, tailored to your businesses needs
